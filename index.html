Who are we?

Momomia is an Indian food chain specializing in momos, a dumpling popular in North-east India, Nepal, and Tibet. The chain was founded in 2018 by Debasish Majumder, a Banker turned Entrepreneur with a passion for food and a dream to create his name in the world.

About MomoMia

MomoMia is one of Bareilly’s favorite hangout spots, known for its delicious momos, fast food, and cozy café vibes. Whether you're meeting friends or grabbing a quick bite, we serve fresh, tasty food at affordable prices.
